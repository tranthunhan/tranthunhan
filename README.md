# About me:
I am a Mechanical Engineering student at the University of Technology Sydney, with a strong interest in mechanical design, robotics, automation, and advanced manufacturing. I enjoy working at the intersection of CAD, prototyping, electronics, and software, particularly where physical systems need to be designed, tested, and improved through iteration.<br>
I am especially interested in robotics, autonomous systems, additive manufacturing, and mechanical products that combine practical design with reliable engineering performance.<br>
Through projects including UTS Motorsports Autonomous, the Warman Challenge robot, capstone robotics work, and design engineering at Armor Road Cases, I have developed skills in SolidWorks, Fusion 360, 3D printing, fabrication, technical documentation, and mechatronic system integration.<br>
My long-term goal is to work in robotics, automation, mechanical design, or product development, where careful design, analysis, prototyping, and continuous improvement are central to the work.
